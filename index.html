Everything you need to know about offshore Ruby on Rails development.

Offshore Ruby on Rails development involves outsourcing your web application development to a Ruby on Rails development company located in a different country. This approach can offer cost savings, access to a global talent pool of skilled Ruby developers, and the flexibility to scale your development team based on project needs. Many successful companies like GitHub, Shopify, and Airbnb were built with Ruby on Rails.
Companies often save on development costs by hiring offshore Ruby on Rails developers. While senior RoR developers in the US typically charge $100-200/hour, offshore Ruby developers may be available at lower rates. Common ranges include $45-75/hour for senior developers, $25-45/hour for mid-level, and $15-25/hour for junior developers. Actual rates vary by location and experience.
Quality assurance starts with choosing the right Ruby on Rails development firm. Look for companies with strong portfolios, verified client testimonials, and clear development processes. Key practices include: code reviews, test-driven development (TDD), continuous integration, regular demos and sprint reviews, and clear communication channels. We only connect you with pre-vetted RoR development companies that meet strict quality standards.
Offshore Ruby on Rails development teams are available across all major time zones. Many companies offer overlapping hours with US/UK business hours (typically 4-6 hours of overlap). Some teams work US hours for real-time collaboration. The key is establishing clear communication schedules and using project management tools like Jira, Slack, and Zoom to maintain seamless collaboration.
